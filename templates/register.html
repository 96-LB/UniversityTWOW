Degree Requirements

In order to graduate UniversityTWOW with a degree in a specific major, one must pass both classes in that department AND earn a total of five credits. In addition, the following major-specific requirements must be met:
  • TWOW Design: Must also take courses in two of the following areas: Sociology, Visual Arts, Mathematics
  • Sociology: Must also take a course in Cultural Studies and a course in either Mathematics or TWOW Design
  • Cultural Studies: Must also take a course in Sociology and a course in either TWOW Design or Visual Arts
  • Visual Arts: Must also take two courses in either Sociology or Cultural Studies
  • Mathematics: Must also take a course in TWOW Design and a course in either Cultural Studies or Visual Arts
Multiple degrees may be attained through completion of the criteria for multiple majors as long as the student has passed and earned credit for at least five classes.
